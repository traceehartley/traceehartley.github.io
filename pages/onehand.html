How to Crack an Egg
One Handed Crack

Step One

Hit middle of egg on the side of bowl with preferred hand until it breaks.

Step Two

Bring egg over the middle of bowl and begin applying pressure to the point of breakage with your thumb, ring finger, and pinky. Hold top of egg with middle and index finger.

Step Three

Begin to pull egg shell in opposite directions with fingers until the yolk falls into the bowl.

Step Four

Gently shake egg shell to remove access egg white from the shell and into the bowl.
