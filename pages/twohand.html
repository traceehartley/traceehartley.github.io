How to Crack an Egg
One Handed Crack

Step One

Hit middle of egg on the side of bowl with preferred hand until it breaks.

Step Two

Bring egg over the middle of bowl and hold with two hands. Begin applying pressure to the point of breakage with both of your thumbs.

Step Three

Pull egg shell in opposite directions with thumbs and turn point of break towards the bowl until the egg falls from gravity.

Step Four

Gently shake egg shell to remove access egg white from the shell and into the bowl.
